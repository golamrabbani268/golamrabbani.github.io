About

I am Golam Rabbani, currently an undergraduate at Yale-NUS College in Singapore. I like to think of myself as a curious lifelong student with a special interest in marine biology. When I'm not working or reading (both of which take up a small fraction of my days), I like to spend my time on productive and knowledgeable platforms, such as Youtube, cycling around the city, or playing board games.
